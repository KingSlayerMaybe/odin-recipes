Sandwich

Description

A delicious sandwich with fresh vegetables and cheese between two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter both bread slices
  2. Layer cheese and vegetables
  3. Press both slices together
  4. Toast on a pan for 2 minutes each side
